Our Story

From Quimigrate to Quimigrate.AI

From a manual form-filling tool to an AI-powered assistant trusted by immigration professionals.

Our Journey

The Evolution

Quimigrate.AI was born out of Quimigrate, a platform originally designed to help individuals navigate the labyrinth of U.S. immigration paperwork. What started as a simple form template library quickly revealed a deeper problem: the forms themselves were only half the battle.

Understanding which form to file, how to answer each question correctly, and what supporting evidence to include was where applicants—and even experienced attorneys—spent most of their time. We realized that a conversational AI could dramatically reduce this friction.

By embedding legal knowledge directly into an interactive chatbot, we transformed a static library into a dynamic assistant. Today, Quimigrate.AI handles everything from initial eligibility screening to final form generation, serving both self-filing individuals and high-volume immigration law firms.

Security First

Data Privacy & Compliance

Immigration data is among the most sensitive information an individual can share. We treat it that way. Quimigrate.AI is built on a foundation of strict data isolation, end-to-end encryption, and zero-retention policies for conversational inputs once a case is finalized.

We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully compliant with ABA Model Rules regarding client confidentiality. All data is stored in U.S.-based, encrypted data centers, and we never use client information to train our models.
